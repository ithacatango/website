Welcome to the online home of Ithaca Tangueros!

We are a student organization at Cornell University. We organize events for Argentine tango dancers at Cornell and the greater Ithaca area, including informal practices every Thursday, tango parties (milongas) every Sunday and occasional workshops with local and visiting instructors. We also maintain a mailing list, an events calendar and this website, with information about more tango events in Ithaca and upstate NY. 

We do not dance competitively, we dance for fun! Check out this blog post for some general info about Argentine tango. If you like what you see, why not give it a try? We love new dancers, and tango is for everyone, young and old, introvert and extrovert, regardless of fitness level, talent or previous dance experience. Here are some ways to get started:

  • Free informal drop-in intro lessons occasionally offered before our Thursday practices, 7-8 pm, Physical Sciences Building (but see the calendar in case the location changes). These are informal, non-progressive classes taught by a rotating cast of our more experienced dancers. They can be a great way to see what tango is all about, before committing to a series to really solidify your knowledge. Note: some weeks there might not be a volunteer available. To be sure the intro lessons are on, send us an email or sign up for our weekly emails (instructions below).

  • Progressive series of classes with local instructor Juan Brache. Lessons once a week on Mondays, at Just Because Center, located at 1013 W State St, Ithaca. $60 per person for a series of 4 consecutive classes, $45 for students, $20 per drop-in lesson. Lessons are on pause for the Summer. Check back for updates!

  • Cornell PE class, Fall session or Spring session. Once a week, FREE and open to the general public (people not affiliated with Cornell University). See www.pe.cornell.edu/classes for more details.

Or just drop by our Thursday practices or Sunday tango parties to see what tango is all about. And feel free to contact us if you have any questions!

Beginner Series:

Next beginner lesson 4-week series level I with Juan Brache begins February 5, 2018 at Just Because Center, 1013 W State St, Ithaca, from 5:30-6:45pm.

Intermediate Series:

For students who already have a grasp of the basic fundamentals, Juan Brache teaches occasional Intermediate series covering specific topics in more depth. These take place at Just Because Center, 1013 W State St, Ithaca, from 8-9:15pm. Join our list serve to hear about the next series that is offered.

Drop-in intro lessons:

Curious about tango, but not sure you want to commit to a series yet? Come to our Thursday practices! Take a free intro class, 7-8pm, then stay to practice what you've learned. See calendar for times and locations and sign up to our listserv to receive updates.
