Lessons

Beginner Series:

Next beginner lesson 4-week series level I with Juan Brache begins February 5, 2018 at Just Because Center, 1013 W State St, Ithaca, from 5:30-6:45pm.

Intermediate Series:

For students who already have a grasp of the basic fundamentals, Juan Brache teaches occasional Intermediate series covering specific topics in more depth. These take place at Just Because Center, 1013 W State St, Ithaca, from 8-9:15pm. Join our list serve to hear about the next series that is offered.

Drop-in intro lessons:

Curious about tango, but not sure you want to commit to a series yet? Come to our Thursday practices! Take a free intro class, 7-8pm, then stay to practice what you've learned. See calendar for times and locations and sign up to our listserv to receive updates.
